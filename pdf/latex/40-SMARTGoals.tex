\hypertarget{primary-goal-focus}{%
\paragraph{Primary Goal Focus}\label{primary-goal-focus}}

S

Specific

What exactly do you want to achieve? Be precise and detailed.

M

Measurable

How will you track progress? What numbers will you use?

A

Achievable

Is this realistic given your current resources and constraints?

R

Relevant

Does this align with your core values and long-term vision?

T

Time-Bound

When is the deadline? What are your milestones?
